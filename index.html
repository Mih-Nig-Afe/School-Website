Welcome To Saint Daniel Comboni

Saint Daniel Comboni School, located in Hawassa, Ethiopia, is a Catholic institution that provides quality education to students from diverse backgrounds. The school is named after Saint Daniel Comboni, a 19th-century Italian Catholic priest who dedicated his life to serving the people of Africa. The school offers a well-rounded education, with a curriculum that covers a variety of subjects including mathematics, science, history, and language arts. In addition to academic subjects, the school also places a strong emphasis on character development and values education. This includes promoting a sense of community, respect for others, and a commitment to social justice. One of the unique features of Saint Daniel Comboni School is its commitment to serving the local community. The school provides educational opportunities to children from disadvantaged backgrounds and works to bridge the gap between different cultures and backgrounds. In addition to providing quality education, the school also supports the local community through various outreach programs, such as providing access to clean water, health care, and job training. The faculty and staff at Saint Daniel Comboni School are highly qualified and dedicated to helping students reach their full potential. They are committed to providing a supportive and challenging learning environment that encourages students to take risks and stretch their boundaries. The school also provides a range of extracurricular activities, including sports, music, and the arts, to help students develop their skills and interests outside of the classroom. In conclusion, Saint Daniel Comboni School in Hawassa is a Catholic institution that is dedicated to providing quality education and serving the local community. The school offers a well-rounded education, with a strong emphasis on character development and values education. The faculty and staff are highly qualified and committed to helping students reach their full potential, and the school provides a range of extracurricular activities to help students develop their skills and interests.

What do we AIM for?



The CSPS School is dedicated to fostering a supportive and inclusive learning environment that promotes academic and personal growth for all students. We strive to instill a strong foundation of behavioral skills and educate our students to become confident, responsible, and respectful individuals. Our mission is to provide a challenging and dynamic curriculum that prepares students for success in a rapidly changing world.

The vision for the CSPS School is to create a safe and nurturing learning environment where every student is empowered to reach their full potential through a combination of behavior-based and education-based approaches. We aim to cultivate a community of lifelong learners who are equipped with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in a rapidly changing world.
